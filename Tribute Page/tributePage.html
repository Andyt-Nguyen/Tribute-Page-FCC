The Music

"Of Monsters And Men" was formed in 2009 and they are categorized as 'indie folk pop'. Their first album "My Head Is An Animal" was developed in 2011. This album has a very folky feel to it, I say this not because there categorized as folk, but in their future album "Beneath The Skin" it has no folky-ness to it at all. Anyways "My Head Is An Animal" has a ton of story telling and quite frankly, that is what really separates them from a lot of bands that we hear on the radio. The lyrics within every song has a fictional story incorporated in it such as animals, kings, queens, monsters etc. which makes the songs really awesome to listen and visualize to. "Beneath The Skin," their second album, took a different direction from that album. It has more of a darker ambiance to it. This album is about self-development, dealing with anxeity, and fighting the need to change who you are to fit in society; other than that still a pretty damn good album. If you like to know more about this awesome band checkout their Youtube channel or if you want to know who's in it or their history check out their wiki.
